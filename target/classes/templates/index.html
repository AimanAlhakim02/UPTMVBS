VENUES @ UPTM

Your Exciting Journey Starts Here

Lecture Room

This lecture room is designed for educational sessions and academic presentations, including rows of desks confronting a central podium in a formal environment.

Computer Lab

The lab is equipped with sophisticated computer workstations, serving as a center for technology education and digital discovery.

Childhood Room

This area is specifically created for early childhood education, featuring a lively atmosphere with toys and resources that encourage learning and play for young children.

Library

The library provides a peaceful setting for research, study, and reading, serving as a haven of knowledge.

Mac Lab

The Mac lab, equipped with stylish Apple computers, serves as a hub for fostering creativity in digital arts and computing proficiency.

Music Room

This area is designed for musicians to rehearse, play music, and be surrounded by instruments and sound equipment in an acoustically optimized environment.

Studio

This studio is well-suited for dancing, rehearsals, and many types of movement arts due to its mirrored walls and ample floor space.

Surau

The surau offers a tranquil space for spiritual activities, contemplation, and prayer within the establishment.

Futsal Court

This outdoor futsal court is open-air and provides a dynamic setting for students to participate in soccer matches and experience the excitement of outdoor sports.
